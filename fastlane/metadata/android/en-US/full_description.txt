<b>Driftly is a fully-featured Attendance Tracker app with Material Design 3 UI, letting you save your daily attendance and analyze them, packed in a beautiful and smart interface</b>

<b>Features</b>

- 🎨 Modern Material Design 3 UI

- 📲 Save attendance

- 🕵️Progress summarization

- 🗓️ Inbuilt Calendar

- 🛠️ Support for Adding, Deleting or Editing

- 🌑 AMOLED-Friendly Dark Theme

- 🎉 Packed with Extras
